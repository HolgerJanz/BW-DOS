Highspeed SIO patch 1.33 XL/XE/MyIDE
by Hias

The patch modifies the OS ROM so that
highspeed SIO capable disk drives and
also emulators like AtariSIO, SIO2PC,
APE, etc. will be accessed in full
speed.

It patches the currently active OS
which needs the RAM under the OS ROM.
